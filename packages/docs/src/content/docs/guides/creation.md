### Creating an Account

1. Navigate to www.capsule.sh/signup
2. Fill in required information:
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Validate the CAPTCHA
3. Click on the link you received by email
4. Wait until the capsule is finished mounting

### Logging In

1. Go to www.ui.capsule.sh
2. Enter your credentials
